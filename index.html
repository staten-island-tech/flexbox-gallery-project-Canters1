Some of My Favorite Albums

(No particular order)

My Beautiful Dark Twisted Fantasy

This is my second favorite Kanye album, after Graduation (obv.). My favorite songs off the album are "POWER, "All Of The Lights", and "Runaway". POWER was the first song in my main playlist for like 2 years, so its still my most streamed song by far.

Call Me If You Get Lost

When I first listened to this album I didn't really like it. Now it might be my favorite abum of all time. I like pretty much every song on the album, but my three favorites are probably "RISE!", "SAFARI", and "CORSO". Tyler is probably my 3rd or 4th favorite artist right now.

Heroes & Villains

Like many other albums, my brother introduced me to Heroes & Villains by playing it loudly on his speaker at night. I got into the rest of Metro Boomin's work through this album. My three favorite songs are: "On Time", "Superhero", and "Feel The Fiyaaaah"

Graduation

My three favorite songs off the album are "Homecoming", "The Glory", and "Homecoming", though I like literally every song on the album except Drunk and Hot Girls. This is probably my favorite album of all time. I need to reach another line to make it look right.
